# HBS Compliance Manager - Project Charter

**Version:** 10.1 Development  
**Baseline:** Last working uploaded ZIP  
**Target Production Release:** v11.0

## Project vision

To create a focused commercial gas compliance management system for HBS that controls engineer audits, competency actions, training, knowledge records, re-audits and compliance reporting from one secure web platform.

## Core principle

The platform is not an HR database and must not become one. It manages compliance activity and audit-generated performance only.

## Explicit exclusions

The following features are out of scope and are not to be developed in Engineer Management or Administration:

- Engineer profile pages
- Qualifications
- ACS expiry
- LPG records
- OFTEC records
- G3 records
- Driving licence checks
- Medical restrictions
- Regional allocation
- General HR or personnel files

## Approved modules

### 1. Dashboard

- Company compliance KPIs
- Audit statistics
- Failed audits
- Re-audits due
- Open training
- Competency reviews
- Recent activity

### 2. Audit Centre

- Conduct audits
- Record findings
- Record safety and combustion readings
- Generate scores and outcomes
- Save audit records

### 3. Audit History

- Search and review previous audits
- View audit detail
- Review findings, questions and readings
- Access photo evidence where stored

### 4. Engineer Performance & Competency

This section is generated from operational compliance data only:

- Audit history
- Average audit score
- Failed audit count
- Open training
- Re-audit status
- Competency status
- Recommended action

### 5. Training Centre

- Assigned training
- Toolbox talks
- Online tests
- Completion status
- Manager sign-off

### 6. Knowledge Centre

- Technical bulletins
- Manufacturer information
- IGEM/Gas Safe guidance references
- Company procedures
- Uploaded library records

### 7. Reports & Analytics

- Engineer league tables
- Audit trends
- Compliance KPIs
- Training status
- Re-audit status
- Exportable records where required

### 8. Administration

- Users
- Roles
- Permissions
- Record management
- Backup
- System configuration

Administration is limited to system access and operational control. It must not be expanded into HR/employee record management.
